About me

I am a Hungarian woman from Yugoslavia. I was born in Subotica on 16.05.1995.. I wanted to be a veterinarian as a child, but I was afraid of animals. For a long time I didn't know what I wanted to be when I grew up. Finally I decided in high school, that I wanted to work with children. Currently I am a special education teacher in Szeged. I love children, I love teaching, but I realized that this is not my path. A few years ago I decided to become a frontend developer. Some of my hobbies: hiking, cycling and strategy boardgames, like Azul, Catan, Carcassone.
